Sales Trend Analysis

Python · pandas · matplotlib · seaborn 2026

Overview

A regional retailer needed to understand 3-year sales trends to inform inventory planning and promotional strategy. This project performs end-to-end exploratory data analysis on transactional sales data, surfaces seasonality patterns, and visualizes key revenue drivers.

Methodology

  • Loaded and cleaned raw sales CSV data using pandas — handled missing values, date parsing, and duplicate entries
  • Aggregated sales by month, product category, and region using groupby operations
  • Identified seasonal peaks and troughs with time-series decomposition
  • Visualized trends using matplotlib and seaborn — line charts, heatmaps, and bar plots
